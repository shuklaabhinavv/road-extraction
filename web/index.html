roadx

Roads, extracted from the sky — anywhere.

Pan the map to any place on Earth and extract its road network from live satellite imagery, or upload your own aerial photo. Four segmentation models, trained identically on the Massachusetts Roads Dataset.

Zoom to street level (the button enables when the view is small enough), then extract. Roads are detected on live Esri satellite imagery at ~1 m/pixel — the resolution the models were trained on.
